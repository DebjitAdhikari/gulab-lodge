About Us

Welcome to Gulab Lodge

Gulab Lodge first opened its doors to the public in 1972 under the leadership of US. From the very beginning, it set a new standard for hospitality in the Darjeeling region, helping to shape the tourism scene in North Bengal.

As the first to introduce a fine dining experience in the area, Gulab Lodge quickly became a beloved destination, known for its exceptional food and warm hospitality. What began as a modest 10-room property has now blossomed into a large and vibrant establishment with over 100 rooms, all thanks to the unwavering support of our guests from across India. At Gulab Lodge, we take pride in continuously enhancing our services while offering affordable stays without compromising on quality.
